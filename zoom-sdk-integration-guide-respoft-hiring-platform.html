Zoom SDK Integration Guide – Respoft Hiring Platform

Purpose

This document explains how Zoom is integrated into the Respoft hiring platform and how clients can use it to conduct interviews.

1. Joining a Zoom Meeting

When an interview is scheduled via Respoft, both the recruiter and candidate receive a link or are directed to a meeting screen embedded in the platform. The Zoom Meeting SDK is used to render the meeting UI directly inside the Respoft platform.

Steps:

  • Login to the Respoft platform
  • Navigate to the “Scheduled Interviews” section
  • Click “Join Meeting” – the meeting loads inside the app interface

2. Starting a Zoom Meeting

Interviewers can start a scheduled meeting via the Respoft dashboard. The system generates the meeting via backend logic and uses the SDK token to authenticate the host.

3. FAQ

Q: Do users need a Zoom account?
A: Only interviewers may need a Zoom host account (managed by your organization). Candidates do not need to log in.

Q: Is Zoom content recorded or stored?
A: No, Respoft does not store any Zoom audio, video, or chat content.

Q: Who can I contact for support?
A: Email us at info@respoft.in.
